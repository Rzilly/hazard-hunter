About

I build safe cars, or more precisely, I make sure the electronics inside them don’t do anything they’re not supposed to. With 4+ years in Functional Safety and E-Mobility, I’ve worked from concept all the way to vehicle integration, ensuring safety is designed in, tested, and signed off. I’m a TÜV SÜD Certified Functional Safety Engineer (ISO 26262:2018) with hands-on experience in hazard analysis, safety concepts, safety mechanism validation, and ASPICE-aligned development.I’ve collaborated with OEMs like Volkswagen, Hyundai, and Daimler on Mild-Hybrid, High-Voltage Drive, and ADAS projects — from safety work products and requirements engineering to HIL/SIL/MIL testing and calibration. .
